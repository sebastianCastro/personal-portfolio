Loops, Layers, and Late Nights

I got into music production at the end of elementary school, inspired by mainstream DJs. I started out on a cracked copy of FL Studio, obsessively learning how tracks were built. Discovering Skrillex later on completely shifted my world — I switched to Ableton Live, thinking 'if it's good enough for him, it must be the best.' (Spoiler: it’s never just about the software.) Since then, making music has been a constant — I’d come home from school and lose hours crafting sounds. It’s still my favorite way to unwind and create.
